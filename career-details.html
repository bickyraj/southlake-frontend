About Southlake Diagnostics Inc

Southlake Diagnostics Inc (a Boston BioPharm Company) is an established and growing provider of Diagnostics and Clinical Trial Services, including and not limited to high-complexity chemistry, immunoassay, and pharmacokinetic assays. Our diagnostic and clinical laboratory has two locations in Southlake Texas, along with manufacturing sites throughout the United States. We are currently seeking highly motivated, results-oriented sales professionals for the following positions:

National Account Manager, Regional Account Manager, and Account Manager to expand the lab's reach across the United States in certain Diagnostic and Clinical Trial Analytics. Earning potential is unlimited for the right candidates. If you are an experienced, self- driven team player, with a strong work ethic and drive to succeed, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities

The primary responsibility of these positions will be business development and management of designated accounts and territories in Texas, the South east/west, and throughout the United States - with the goal of market penetration and growth within targeted groups of Nursing Homes, Assisted Living, Over-55 Communities, Hospitals (regional/national), certain Health Care Providers and Fortune 100 to 500 Companies while:

  • Identifying any and all clients, clinics, or business opportunities that could benefit from our unique laboratory diagnostic and clinical services while expanding current market share.
  • Developing business relationships with key client(s) and target customers.
  • Delivering product presentations to customers in your territory outlining laboratory benefits and services.
  • Achieving quarterly sales goals and meeting and maintaining expectations of the field and limited administrative duties.
  • Understanding and addressing both business and scientific-oriented needs of companies in the drug, dietary ingredient, and healthcare through consistent account engagement and dialogue.

  • Minimum of 3+ years of successful Laboratory Sales experience required.
  • Current Clinical relationships in the market are required, and candidate must be able to demonstrate/document that he/she have successful industry relationships.
  • A Demonstrated ability to listen, learn, and apply technical and scientific product-related information
  • Consistent track record of quantifiable/documented sales accomplishments
  • Solid business acumen in the lab, healthcare, or CRO analytical space, including excellent and persuasive business communication
